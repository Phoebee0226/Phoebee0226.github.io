Poster - Female Exploitation-Social Issue

Poster - Female Exploitation-Social Issue

Project Description
These impactful posters tackle the issue of female exploitation with strong visual narratives. The first features a woman's silhouette manipulated like a puppet, symbolizing control and manipulation. The second shows a woman confined within a hand, representing oppression. Silhouettes create an anonymous representation, emphasizing the collective experience of exploited women. The stark contrast intensifies the sense of vulnerability. The text "Every woman deserves respect, free from sexual exploitation" reinforces the message, advocating for women's dignity. The minimalistic design focuses attention on the gravity of the subject, aiming to evoke empathy and raise awareness about the systemic issue of female exploitation. Overall, the posters convey a powerful call to action against the injustices faced by women globally.
